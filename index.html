Gender Equity Success in Kenyan Primary Schools

This map compares Kenyan primary school enrollments for grades 1-8 by gender for the year 2014 and shows enrollment distributions by county. Use the time slider to change grade year. Hover over each county for more information. Gender parity appears generally greater at the lower grade levels and in the southwestern side of the country.

About the data

The data visualized in this map comes from a dataset named "National Boys and Girls Enrollments numbers per Class for Primary School" and contains enrollment numbers from the year 2014. This data was created by the Kenyan government and accessed via the Kenya Open Data Portal.
